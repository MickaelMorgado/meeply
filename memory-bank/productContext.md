# Product Context

## The Problem

Board game enthusiasts struggle to effectively track, manage, and share their gaming experiences. Current solutions are fragmented:

- **Manual Tracking:** Players use notebooks, spreadsheets, or memory to track games played
- **Collection Management:** No centralized way to catalog and organize game collections
- **Social Disconnect:** Limited ability to connect with other players and share gaming experiences
- **Statistics Gap:** Lack of insights into gaming habits and preferences
- **Discovery Issues:** Difficulty finding new games and learning rules

## The Solution

Meeply addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive mobile companion app that serves as the central hub for board game management and social interaction.

### Core Features

1. **Play Logging:** Easy tracking of game sessions with scores, players, duration, and notes
2. **Collection Management:** Digital catalog of owned games with organization and search capabilities
3. **Statistics & Analytics:** Detailed insights into gaming habits, favorite games, and performance trends
4. **Social Features:** Connect with other players, share experiences, and discover new gaming communities
5. **Rule Learning:** Integrated rule references and tutorials for new games

## User Experience Goals

- **Intuitive Interface:** Clean, mobile-first design that feels native to iOS and Android
- **Quick Actions:** Fast logging and management workflows for busy gaming sessions
- **Visual Appeal:** Engaging design that reflects the fun and social nature of board gaming
- **Accessibility:** Inclusive design that works for all users regardless of experience level

## Market Position

Meeply positions itself as the essential companion for modern board gamers, bridging the gap between traditional tabletop gaming and digital convenience. The landing page serves as the gateway to this ecosystem, converting curious visitors into engaged app users.
